Frequently asked Questions (FAQ)

Q.

Can you walk/reserve your favourite dog

A.

Yes you can, if your profile has the rank of dog-walker then you can reserve any dog you want!

Q.

How do I start?

A.

Just download the app through the google play store or the apple app store - or through the link at the bottom of our website! The app will give you information on how to set everything up.

Q.

What kind of dogs can I walk with?

A.

You can check all the different kind of dogs on the app or by visiting a local shelter.
