Preview

When you open the app, you will be asked to log in or register a new account.

Here you can choose a location and see which dogs are available with a short description of each dog. You can also click on the button at the bottom to see the rewards page.

Depending on how many points you have achieved, you can choose between many different kinds of rewards to collect.

After finishing a walk you will see your stats, how long and how far you've walked and how many points you got.
